WINDSTORM OR HAIL EXCLUSION

 

 

 

This policy does not insure against loss caused directly or indirectly by windstorm or hail to the exterior of the following building:

 

 

            DESCRIPTION OF BUILDINGS(S)                                               LOCATION

 

                                                                                        

 

 

 

 

 

 

However, “we” pay for direct loss by fire or explosion to the exterior of these excluded buildings, if the fire or explosion results from windstorm or hail.  “We” pay only for the damage caused directly by the fire or explosion.

 

In the event that the exterior of any of the buildings listed on this exclusion are repaired or improved, you must follow proper underwriting procedures in order to have the exclusion removed.  Please contact your agent upon completion of any repairs or improvements.

 

This exclusion does not apply to Coverage D, or to shingles.  Shingle exclusion, if applicable, are part of a separate exclusion.
